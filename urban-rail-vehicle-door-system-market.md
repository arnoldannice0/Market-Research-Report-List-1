<p><p>The Urban Rail Vehicle Door System market comprises three main types: electric, pneumatic, and manual systems. Electric door systems utilize motors for seamless operation, offering quick, reliable access and automation, which is essential for modern urban transport. Pneumatic systems rely on compressed air mechanisms, providing robust performance ideal for heavy-duty applications. Manual systems, while less common in modern designs, are operated by hand and can be found in older vehicles, offering simplicity and low maintenance. Each type serves distinct operational needs in urban rail environments.</p></p>
