<p><p>The LED driver market includes two primary types: constant voltage and constant current drivers. Constant voltage drivers supply a consistent voltage level, compatible with multiple LED configurations, making them ideal for applications where uniform brightness is essential. In contrast, constant current drivers regulate the current flowing through the LEDs, ensuring optimal performance and longer lifespan, particularly for high-power applications. Each type caters to different needs, influencing efficiency, brightness stability, and overall lighting quality in various settings.</p></p>
