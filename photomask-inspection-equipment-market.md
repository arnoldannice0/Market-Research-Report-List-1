<p><strong>The Photomask Inspection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photomask inspection equipment is crucial for ensuring the fidelity of photomasks used in semiconductor manufacturing. The Die to Die (DD) method inspects individual die on a photomask by comparing features within the same mask, focusing on local defect detection. In contrast, the Die to Database (DB) method compares the die features against a pre-established database of reference patterns, allowing for a broader analysis of discrepancies. Both methods aim to enhance the quality and reliability of photomasks in high-precision fabrication processes.</p></p>
