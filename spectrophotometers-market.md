<p><ul><li>UV-Visible Spectrometry</li><li>IR- Spectrometry</li><li>Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR)</li><li>Near Infrared Spectrometry</li><li>Raman Spectrometry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spectrophotometer market encompasses various types, each serving distinct analytical needs. UV-Visible spectrometry analyzes light absorption in the ultraviolet and visible range, commonly used in chemical and biological applications. IR spectrometry detects molecular vibrations to identify compounds. Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR) utilizes magnetic fields to determine molecular structures. Near Infrared spectrometry measures overtones and combinations of molecular vibrations, while Raman spectrometry analyzes inelastic scattering of light to provide molecular insights. These techniques play crucial roles in research, quality control, and material analysis across industries.</p></p>
