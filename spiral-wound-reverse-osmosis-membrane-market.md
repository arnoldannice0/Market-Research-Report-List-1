<p><p>The Spiral Wound Reverse Osmosis (RO) Membrane market includes two primary types: Composite Membranes and Asymmetric Membranes. Composite Membranes consist of multiple layers, with a thin, selective polyamide layer on a porous support, offering high salt rejection and durability. Asymmetric Membranes have a single polymeric material with a dense top layer and a porous sub-layer, providing efficient filtration. Both types are widely used in water purification, desalination, and industrial processes, catering to diverse applications based on performance, cost, and operational requirements.</p></p>
