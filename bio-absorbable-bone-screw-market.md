<p><p>The bio-absorbable bone screw market features two primary types: fully threaded screws and partially threaded screws. Fully threaded screws have threads that cover the entire length, providing superior anchorage and mechanical stability in various applications. In contrast, partially threaded screws have threads only along a portion of their length, offering flexibility and ease of implantation. Both types are designed to support bone healing and gradually dissolve in the body, eliminating the need for subsequent hardware removal while minimizing long-term complications.</p></p>
