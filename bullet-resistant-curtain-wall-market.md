<p><p>The bullet-resistant curtain wall market comprises two primary types: unitized and stick curtain walls. Unitized curtain walls are pre-constructed panels assembled in a factory, allowing for rapid installation and consistent quality. They offer enhanced protection and aesthetic appeal. In contrast, stick curtain walls are assembled on-site using individual components, providing flexibility in design. This type may take longer to install but allows for customization. Both types are essential in security-focused buildings, serving as effective barriers against ballistic threats.</p></p>
