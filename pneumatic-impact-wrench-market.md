<p><p>The pneumatic impact wrench market consists primarily of two main types: impact wrenches and pulse pneumatic wrenches. Impact wrenches deliver a series of strong, quick torque bursts, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications in automotive and construction industries. Pulse pneumatic wrenches, on the other hand, utilize a unique mechanism that provides controlled torque output, enhancing precision and reducing the risk of damage to fasteners. Both types leverage compressed air to achieve high power and efficiency in fastening tasks.</p></p>
