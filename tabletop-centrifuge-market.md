<p><p>The tabletop centrifuge market is divided into two main types: low speed and high speed. High-speed centrifuges, operating above 5,000 RPM, are designed for more demanding applications, such as isolating cellular components and DNA extraction in research and clinical settings. Both types serve critical roles across healthcare, research, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
