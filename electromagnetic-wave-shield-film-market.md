<p><p>The electromagnetic wave shield film market includes two primary types: metal-mesh film and coating film. Coating films, on the other hand, consist of specialized materials applied to surfaces, providing a protective layer against electromagnetic waves without compromising aesthetics. Both types serve critical applications in electronics, telecommunications, and automotive industries, enhancing device performance by safeguarding against unwanted electromagnetic radiation.</p></p>
