<p><strong>The Small Distribution Transformers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pole-mounted Transformers</li><li>Pad-mounted Transformers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small distribution transformers market comprises various types, notably pole-mounted and pad-mounted transformers. Pole-mounted transformers are typically installed on utility poles in outdoor settings, providing localized voltage transformation for overhead distribution systems. They are compact, cost-effective, and commonly used in rural areas. In contrast, pad-mounted transformers are ground-mounted units, enclosed in secure metal cabinets, ideal for urban environments. They cater to underground distribution networks, offering aesthetic appeal and reducing space requirements while ensuring safety and accessibility for maintenance.</p></p>
