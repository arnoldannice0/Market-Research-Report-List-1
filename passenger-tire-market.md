<p><strong>The Passenger Tire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Tire</li><li>Bias Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The passenger tire market primarily consists of radial and bias tires. Radial tires feature layers of fabric or steel cords arranged perpendicular to the direction of travel, offering better fuel efficiency, durability, and grip. They dominate the market due to their performance advantages. Bias tires, with crisscrossed cord layers, are less common but provide sturdiness and load-bearing capacity, often used in specific applications like off-road or vintage vehicles. Radial tires are preferred for modern passenger vehicles, while bias tires cater to niche markets.</p></p>
