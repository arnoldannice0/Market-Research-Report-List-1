<p><p>LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic) couplers are essential components in the telecommunications and RF markets, facilitating signal division and combining. Directional couplers allow signal flow in one primary direction, useful for monitoring and signal sampling without affecting the main path. In contrast, dual-directional couplers enable signal flow in both directions, making them ideal for applications like distributed antenna systems where signals are transmitted and received simultaneously. Both types enhance system performance while ensuring minimal signal loss and interference.</p></p>
