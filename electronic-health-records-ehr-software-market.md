<p><p>The Electronic Health Records (EHR) software market is divided into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based EHR systems store data on remote servers, offering greater accessibility and scalability, while reducing hardware costs. They are ideal for practices looking for flexibility and easy updates. In contrast, on-premises systems require installation on local servers, providing complete control over data and security but necessitating significant infrastructure investment and ongoing maintenance. Each type caters to different organizational needs and resources.</p></p>
