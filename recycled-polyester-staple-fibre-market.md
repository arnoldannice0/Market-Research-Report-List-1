<p><p>The recycled polyester staple fiber market is segmented into two main types: solid fiber and hollow fiber. Solid fiber is dense and provides excellent strength, making it ideal for applications like textiles and insulation. In contrast, hollow fiber features a tubular structure that enhances thermal insulation and lightweight properties, catering to products like apparel and bedding. Both types contribute to sustainability by repurposing plastic waste, helping to reduce environmental impact while meeting diverse consumer needs in the textile industry.</p></p>
