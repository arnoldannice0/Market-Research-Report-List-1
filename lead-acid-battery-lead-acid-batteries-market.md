<p><ul><li>VRLA Battery</li><li>Flooded Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lead-acid batteries are widely used for energy storage and provide reliable power supply for various applications. The market is primarily segmented into two types: VRLA (Valved Regulated Lead-Acid) batteries and flooded batteries. VRLA batteries are sealed and require minimal maintenance, offering advantages in portability and spill resistance, making them ideal for UPS and renewable energy systems. In contrast, flooded batteries have liquid electrolyte and require regular maintenance, commonly used in automotive and industrial applications due to their lower cost and robustness.</p></p>
