<p><strong>The Carded Blister Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoforming</li><li>Cold forming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carded blister packaging is designed to showcase and protect products within a transparent plastic bubble, affixed to a cardstock backing. The market includes two primary types: thermoforming and cold forming. Thermoforming involves heating plastic sheets, molding them into blisters, and sealing them to cardstock, providing a cost-effective solution for various items. Cold forming utilizes a thicker, more robust aluminum foil, offering superior protection and barrier properties, making it ideal for pharmaceuticals and sensitive products, enhancing shelf life and security.</p></p>
