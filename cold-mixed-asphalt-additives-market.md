<p><p>The Cold Mixed Asphalt Additives market includes various types that enhance performance and durability. Polymeric modifiers improve elasticity and resistance to deformation. Anti-strip and adhesion promoters enhance bonding between asphalt and aggregates, preventing water damage. Emulsifiers facilitate the mixing of asphalt with water, lowering temperatures required for application. Chemical modifiers alter the chemical properties of asphalt for better resistance and performance. Other additives may include fillers and additives that further improve specific traits of the asphalt mix.</p></p>
