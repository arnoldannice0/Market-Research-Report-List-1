<p><p>The Serum Separating Tubes (SST) market primarily serves two applications: venous blood collection and capillary blood collection. In venous blood collection, SSTs facilitate efficient separation of serum from blood after centrifugation, ensuring high-quality samples for laboratory testing. Meanwhile, in capillary blood collection, SSTs are utilized for smaller sample volumes, often obtained via fingerstick, providing a convenient and efficient alternative for point-of-care testing. Both applications are crucial for accurate diagnostics and monitoring in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
