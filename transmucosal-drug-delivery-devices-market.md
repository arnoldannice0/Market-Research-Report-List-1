<p><p>Transmucosal drug delivery devices facilitate the absorption of medications through mucosal surfaces, enhancing bioavailability and effectiveness. The rectal route is suitable for patients unable to take oral medications, allowing drugs to bypass the digestive system. Each route offers unique advantages based on patient needs and drug properties.</p></p>
