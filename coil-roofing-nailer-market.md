<p><p>The coil roofing nailer market is divided into two main types: corded and cordless models. Corded coil roofing nailers are powered by electricity and are ideal for consistent, continuous use in fixed locations, providing unlimited runtime as long as there is access to a power source. In contrast, cordless coil roofing nailers operate on batteries, offering greater mobility and flexibility, allowing users to work in areas without power access. Both types are designed for efficiency in roofing applications, enhancing productivity.</p></p>
