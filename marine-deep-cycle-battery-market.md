<p><p>The Marine Deep Cycle Battery market features primarily two types: Flooded Lead Acid Batteries and Lithium Ion Batteries. Flooded Lead Acid Batteries are cost-effective and reliable, using liquid electrolyte, making them suitable for traditional marine applications. However, they require regular maintenance. In contrast, Lithium Ion Batteries offer higher energy density, longer lifespan, and faster charging capabilities, making them increasingly popular for modern marine vessels. Their lightweight and compact design enhances vessel performance and efficiency, driving their demand in the market.</p></p>
