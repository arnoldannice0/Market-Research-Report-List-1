<p><p>The **Antiserum Market** includes various types tailored to combat specific pathogens or conditions. **Antitoxin Serum** neutralizes toxins from bacterial infections like tetanus or diphtheria. **Antibacterial Serum** targets bacterial infections by providing antibodies against specific bacteria. **Antiviral Serum** is designed to fight viral infections by delivering antibodies against viruses like rabies or hepatitis. **Anti-Rh Serum** prevents Rh incompatibility in pregnancies by neutralizing Rh-positive blood cells in Rh-negative mothers. Each type serves a unique therapeutic purpose, addressing toxins, bacteria, viruses, or immune-related conditions.</p></p>
