<p><ul><li>Wet Process LMO</li><li>Dry Process LMO</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LMO (Lithium Manganese Oxide) battery materials market can be divided into two key types: Wet Process LMO and Dry Process LMO. The Wet Process involves dissolving lithium and manganese compounds in a solvent, leading to a uniform mixture that enhances electrochemical properties. In contrast, the Dry Process employs dry powders to create LMO materials, offering advantages in production efficiency and reduced environmental impact. Both methods are crucial for developing high-performance batteries used in electric vehicles and energy storage systems.</p></p>
