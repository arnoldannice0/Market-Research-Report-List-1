<p><p>Traffic management market encompasses various systems to enhance urban mobility. Urban Traffic Management and Control (UTMC) systems optimize traffic flow in cities. Adaptive Traffic Control Systems (ATCS) adjust signal timings in real-time based on traffic conditions. Journey Time Measurement Systems (JTMS) track travel times to provide data for efficiency improvements. Predictive Traffic Modeling Systems (PTMS) forecast traffic patterns to aid planning. Incident Detection and Location Systems (IDLS) quickly identify and locate traffic incidents. Dynamic Traffic Management Systems (DTMS) adaptively manage congestion through variable messaging and routing solutions.</p></p>
