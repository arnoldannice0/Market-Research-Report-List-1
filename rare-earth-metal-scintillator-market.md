<p><ul><li>Sodium Iodide (NaI)</li><li>Cesium Iodide (CsI)</li><li>Lutetium Oxyorthosilicate (LSO) and Lutetium-yttrium Oxyorthosilicate (LYSO)</li><li>Bismuth Germanate Oxide (BGO)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rare Earth Metal Scintillator market encompasses various types, each with distinct properties. Sodium Iodide (NaI) is known for its efficient gamma-ray detection but is hygroscopic. Cesium Iodide (CsI) offers better performance and less moisture absorption. Lutetium Oxyorthosilicate (LSO) and Lutetium-yttrium Oxyorthosilicate (LYSO) provide high energy resolution and fast response times, making them ideal for advanced imaging applications. Bismuth Germanate Oxide (BGO) is recognized for its high density and effective gamma-ray detection. Other variants also exist, catering to specific needs and applications.</p></p>
