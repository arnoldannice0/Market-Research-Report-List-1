<p><p>The Wireless IoT Sensors market features two primary types: Traditional Wireless Technology and LPWANs (Low Power Wide Area Networks). Traditional Wireless Technology encompasses established protocols like Wi-Fi and Bluetooth, suitable for short-range, high-data rate applications. In contrast, LPWANs target long-range communication with low power consumption, making them ideal for devices requiring less frequent data transmission, such as environmental monitoring and smart agriculture. Both technologies cater to diverse IoT applications, balancing performance and energy efficiency based on specific needs.</p></p>
