<p><ul><li>Multi-leaf Spring</li><li>Mono-leaf Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semi-elliptical leaf spring market includes multi-leaf and mono-leaf springs, both used in vehicle suspension systems. Multi-leaf springs consist of multiple steel layers stacked together, offering high load-bearing capacity and durability, ideal for heavy-duty vehicles like trucks. Mono-leaf springs, made from a single, thicker steel layer, are lighter and provide smoother rides, suitable for lighter vehicles. Both types absorb shocks and distribute weight, but multi-leaf springs prioritize strength, while mono-leaf springs focus on weight reduction and comfort. The choice depends on vehicle requirements and performance needs.</p></p>
