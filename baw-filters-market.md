<p><p>BAW (Bulk Acoustic Wave) filters are essential components in modern communication systems, categorized mainly into FBAR (Film Bulk Acoustic Resonator) and SMR (Solidly Mounted Resonator) types. FBAR filters offer high performance with compact size, making them suitable for mobile devices and LTE applications. In contrast, SMR filters provide superior thermal stability and lower insertion loss, ideal for various RF applications. Both types are critical for enhancing signal clarity and minimizing interference in increasingly complex telecommunications environments.</p></p>
